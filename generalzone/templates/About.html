About Us

Our project: E-webgo

Our project E-webgo (Multi level marketing) is a web application which design and develop by kiot students. Our team members contribute their efforts to develop this project. This project is developed under the supervision of Diksha mam.

One platform for all.

In this Multi-Level Marketing Project, we provide both e-commerce and study materials , you can buy laptops, mobiles and other products, and we also provide study materials. We provide a way where consumer can purchase the products, services with reasonable price on the internet. In the local market the product as to move from different agencies so the price is very high. In this project we will provide online services to make the sales easier to the registered members.

We also provide an online interface in the form of e-learning that will provide users online learning materials likes books, lectures videos etc.
